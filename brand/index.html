Best Practices

Color Contrast

Ensure text has sufficient contrast with backgrounds. Site-89 Mint (#4EFAAA) pairs well with Onyx Black (#0D0D0E) and Carbon Gray (#1A1A1C) backgrounds.

Accent Usage

Use accent colors strategically. Emergency Red should be reserved for critical alerts and warnings only. Site-89 Mint draws attention to primary actions.

Typography Hierarchy

Use font weights and sizes to establish clear hierarchy. Headings should be bold Montserrat, body text should be regular Roboto. Technical content uses Roboto Mono.

Consistency

Maintain consistency across all digital and print materials. When in doubt, reference this guide and existing Site-89 materials.
