Welcome to Site-89

Follow these steps to get started on your Foundation journey

0% Complete

Create Character Application

Submit your character application. This is your introduction to the community and helps leadership get to know you.

Join Discord Server

Join our Discord to connect with the community, ask questions, and receive updates about your application status.

Download Techniclauncher & Modpack

Download the Techniclauncher and install the modpack for Minecraft. This is required to join the server.

Wait for Application Approval

Your application is under review. Typically, this takes 3 hours or less. During holidays, it may take up to 48 hours. You'll be notified in Discord when approved.

Join the Server & Create Character

Once approved, join the Minecraft server and create your character using this website's character creation system.

Request IO Agent Assistance

Contact Internal Operations (request in chat or wait for one to join during roleplay). An IO Agent will assist you through the onboarding process.

Receive ID & Keycard

Follow your IO Agent's directions to receive your Personnel ID and Keycard. They'll guide you through the onboarding process.

Register Character & Add Bio

Use your Personnel ID in the "Add Character" menu on this website to fully register your character and create your bio.

Explore Your New Role

Congratulations! You're now an Intern with Level 1 clearance. Explore the Foundation, check out the department pages to find where you'd like to focus, and start your adventure!
