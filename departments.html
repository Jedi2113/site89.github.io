Departments
» Administrative Department «

The Administrative Department (AD) oversees the entire site, controlling financial operations, HR, diplomatic affairs, ethical politics, quality inspections, and more. Its goal is to ensure everything runs smoothly, efficiently, and ethically.

» Department of External Operations «

The DEO communicates with Groups of Interest and monitors everything outside the site. It manages Mobile Task Forces and its own internal Military Intelligence Agency.

» Scientific Department «

The Scientific Department (ScD) has multiple divisions specializing in research, anomaly testing, occult studies, and medical oversight. All divisions work to understand anomalies and ensure containment procedures protect the public.

» Security Department «

The Security Department (SD) ensures site security, anomaly containment, and personnel protection. All requests are processed through Security Command to the appropriate subdepartment.
