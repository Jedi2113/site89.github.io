Phase 1 — Construction

Initial development period focused on infrastructure, backend systems, and foundational architecture. Roleplay activity was minimal or nonexistent.

Public awareness was intentionally limited while the project's core stability and scalability were established.

Completed: May–Aug 2024

Phase 2 — FoF Closed Beta

Semi-private rollout designed to test roleplay systems, policies, and site structure with a limited audience.

Focus placed on establishing core departments and onboarding early staff into long-term leadership roles.

Completed: Sep–Nov 2024

Phase 3 — Open Beta

Site-89 reaches public accessibility with stable core systems. Outreach efforts begin in preparation for full release.

A Site Advertising Board is established to coordinate promotion, content production, and organic growth.
