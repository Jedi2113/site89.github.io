Department of External Operations (DEO)
International and interdimensional operations — beyond the site, protecting the world.

» External Operations Hub «

The Department of External Operations is the branch of Site-89, and the foundation as a whole responsible for maintaining international and, at times, interdimensional operations. The department has a wide array of functions dedicated for non-domestic operations, such as intelligence missions, combat expeditions, diplomacy, anomaly investigations, and joint DEO/ScD research expeditions.

Director: Killian Rehwald
Asst. Director: Henk Meijer
Joint Volunteer Field Program

The Security Department and the Department of External Operations host a joint volunteer program for all willing Security Department members to go on field missions and operations. Interested in field work? Learn more through Security Command.

Working in DEO — For Interns & New Personnel

The Department of External Operations offers unique opportunities for those interested in field operations, intelligence gathering, and international coordination. Whether you're drawn to tactical operations or analytical intelligence work, DEO provides comprehensive training and real-world experience.

What You'll Do

  • Assist with mission planning and operational logistics
  • Conduct intelligence analysis and threat assessments
  • Support field operations and tactical deployments
  • Monitor Groups of Interest (GOIs) and Persons of Interest (POIs)
  • Participate in joint DEO/ScD research expeditions
  • Coordinate with international Foundation sites
  • Document field reports and operational debriefs

Skills & Experience

Strong situational awareness, adaptability, clear communication, and the ability to remain calm under pressure are essential. New personnel receive comprehensive training in:

  • Operational planning and mission briefings
  • Intelligence gathering and analysis techniques
  • Field communications protocols
  • Tactical procedures and safety protocols
  • Post-operational reporting and debriefing
  • Collaboration with international teams

Getting Started

DEO welcomes personnel interested in field operations, intelligence work, and international coordination. Training is provided based on your assigned division. Visit the External Operations Office just outside Gate-A to meet with DEO personnel.

Mobile Task Force (MTF)

MTF@site89.scp

MTF Commander: N/A

"The people with the big guns, and special missions."

The Mobile Task Forces are the main intervention teams in case of a large-scale breach, raid, or war between the Foundation and GOIs. They are some of the most elite units within the SCP Foundation, composed of personnel drawn from across the organization to handle specific threats or situations that exceed the capabilities or expertise of regular field personnel.

Location: MTF Snow Outpost, Wait, Where is this?
Access: Visit MTF operations area or use MTF Portal

Intelligence Agency (IA)

IA@site89.scp

IA Director: N/A

"Intelligence Agency, practically just Military intelligence but with better benefits."

The Intelligence Agency operates as the site's primary military intelligence unit, tasked with gathering, analyzing, and acting on information critical to national and site security. Specializing in threat assessment, surveillance, and counterintelligence, the agency monitors Groups of Interest and Persons of Interest.

Location: External Operations Office, just outside Gate-A
Access: Visit DEO Office or use IA Portal
