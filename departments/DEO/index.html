Overview

The Department of External Operations is the branch of Site-89, and the foundation as a whold responsible for maintaining international and, at times, interdimensional operations. The department has a wide array of functions dedicated for non-domestic operations, such as intelligence missions, combat expeditions, diplomacy, anomaly investigations, and joint DEO/ScD research expeditions.

Working in DEO — For Interns

DEO internships offer a mix of analytic and field-support work. Interns may assist with mission planning, intel analysis, logistics, and post-operational reporting. Expect training in communication protocols, mission briefs, and basic fieldcraft under close supervision.

Skills & Experience

Strong situational awareness, clear writing for reports, adaptability, and the ability to follow orders are essential. Interns will learn about operational planning, communications, and collaboration with multiple teams.
