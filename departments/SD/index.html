Security Department (SD)
Ensures site safety, containment, and personnel security — the guardians of Site-89.

Overview

The Security Department maintains site security, coordinates containment operations, and protects personnel. SD handles access control, routine patrols, incident responses, and works closely with other departments to ensure threats are managed effectively.

Working in SD — For Interns

Interns in SD support watch rotations, assist with access control and incident reporting, and take part in drills and training. Expectations include punctuality, procedural adherence, teamwork, and a commitment to safety. Training is provided and ongoing mentorship ensures interns learn proper containment and security procedures.

Skills & Experience

Discipline, reliability, clear communication, and ability to follow protocols are key. Interns will gain experience with incident reporting, routine security operations, and containment support.
