Security Department (SD)
Prevention, Observation, and Safety — The guardians of Site-89.

» Information Central «

The Security Department (SD) is the security wing of Site-89 specializing in the containment of anomalies, security of facilities and personnel, and coordination of site affairs. All inquiries and requests about the SD and its activities are to be processed through Security Command, and passed down to the needed subdepartment.

Director: Alyx M. Andrews
Assistant Director: "Storm"
Joint Volunteer Field Program

The Security Department and the Department of External Operations host a joint volunteer program for all willing Security Department members to go on field missions and operations. Interested in field work? Learn more through Security Command.

Working in SD — For Interns & New Personnel

The Security Department offers opportunities for personnel at all levels, from new recruits to elite specialists. Whether you're interested in standard security operations, intelligence gathering, or specialized tactical response, SD provides comprehensive training and career progression.

What You'll Do

  • Guard secure areas and maintain checkpoint security
  • Monitor and respond to containment breaches
  • Escort valuable research tests and VIP personnel
  • Patrol facilities and conduct routine security sweeps
  • Manage Class-D personnel and handle rogue staff situations
  • Participate in recontainment operations and emergency response
  • Document incidents and maintain security reports

Skills & Experience

Discipline, reliability, situational awareness, and clear communication are essential. New members receive comprehensive training in:

  • Foundation security protocols and containment procedures
  • Firearms handling and safety (permit required)*
  • Emergency response and breach protocols
  • Incident documentation and reporting
  • Team coordination and tactical communication

*Access to firearms is limited for newer recruits and members who fail to perform their duties responsibly. Contact Director A. Andrews for more information on firearms permits.

Getting Started

Security & Containment is open to all facility personnel willing to undergo the Security Department Intro Training or create an appropriate character. Visit the Security Zone on the second floor of the Upper FPZ, just past Tom's Diner, to meet with SD personnel.

Security Command

SD.Cmd@Site89.org

Operations Commander: N/A

Security Command is the Security Department's advisory and control body, operating within the Security Control Room, Site Control Room, and Dispatch Office. This small elite group advises Upper Command on security issues and enacts policies to further the department's goal of Prevention, Observation, and Safety. They control the distribution of Firearms Permits along with IO, ensuring personnel are responsible and licensed to carry firearms.

- Dir. Andrews
Status: Limited to Sergeants and above (Cap: 9 members)

Specialized Divisions Unit (SDU)

SD.SDU@Site89.org

Commander: "Storm"

We live to save lives.

The Specialized Divisions Units of Site-89 are some of the most elite in the entire region. Their main job is to recontain breached SCP objects as needed, High-Value asset recovery, CSI, CBRN, and Diving. We are among some of the few on-site personnel qualified to accomplish this task properly. We also help regain control over any Class-D who may find themselves armed. Being in unit usually indicates you are among the best of the best in terms of on-site operations.

Security & Containment (S&C)

SD.SecCont@Site89.org

Security Chief: N/A

Guns are fun, but always the last option.

Here at the Foundation, things aren't always exactly safe. We here at the Security & Containment Division are in charge of keeping other valued foundation staff safe. We are among the first line of defense in the event of a breach or infiltration. Some of our duties include (but are definitely not limited to) guarding secure areas, dealing with rogue personnel, keeping Class-D in check, guarding checkpoints, patrolling, and escorting valuable tests regarding research. We accomplish these duties through many various ways, but as a general guiding rule, you will need a gun*. In S&C, you will be the first line for security for the site.
