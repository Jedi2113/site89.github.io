Scientific Department (ScD)
Research, testing, and medical oversight — the minds studying anomalies and keeping containment safe.

Overview

The Scientific Department conducts research into anomalous phenomena, develops containment procedures, and oversees experimental testing. ScD divisions include lab research, field testing teams, medical research, and occult studies.

Working in ScD — For Interns

Interns in ScD get hands-on research experience under strict supervision. Tasks may include assisting with experiments, data collection and analysis, compiling test reports, maintaining lab logs, and participating in controlled testing exercises. Rigorous safety protocols and mentorship by senior researchers are central to the role.

Skills & Experience

Curiosity, careful documentation, patience, and an ability to follow laboratory protocols are essential. Interns will develop scientific reasoning, experiment design awareness, and learn formal reporting standards.
