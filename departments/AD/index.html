Administrative Department (AD)
Oversees site management, finance, human resources, and policy — keeping Site-89 running smoothly.

Overview

The Administrative Department serves as the backbone of Site-89's internal governance, overseeing all non-anomalous operations to ensure efficiency, security, and compliance with Foundation protocol. Responsible for personnel management, interdepartmental coordination, budget allocation, ethical oversight, and high-level decision-making, this department operates closely with site leadership to maintain order across the facility. Members of the Administrative Department are often trusted with sensitive information and given broad authority to inspect, audit, and direct the actions of other departments when necessary to preserve operational integrity.

Working in AD — For Interns

AD is an excellent place for interns who enjoy organization, communication, and logistics. Intern duties include assisting with documentation, helping coordinate onboarding, supporting HR tasks, processing reports, and participating in audits and planning meetings. Expect close mentorship, practical experience with site governance, and opportunities to contribute to meaningful projects.

Skills & Experience

Attention to detail, clear written communication, comfort with structured procedures, and the ability to collaborate across teams are highly valued. Interns will gain experience in administrative workflows, policy drafting, and interdepartmental coordination.
